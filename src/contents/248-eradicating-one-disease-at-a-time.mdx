Halfdan Mahler, the Director-General of WHO from 1973 to 1988, describes in [an interview from 2008](http://www.who.int/bulletin/volumes/86/10/08-041008/en/) how they came to promote primary health care to all instead of eradicating one disease at a time. It was declared "a most important world-wide social goal" at the International Conference on Primary Health Care in Alma Ata 1978. The publication is available to download as PDF: [www.gapm.io/xalmaata](http://www.gapm.io/xalmaata).
