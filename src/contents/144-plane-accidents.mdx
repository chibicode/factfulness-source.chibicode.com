The data on fatalities in recent years is from IATA. The data on passenger miles is from the UN agency that managed to reduce the number of accidents, based on ICAO[1,2,3]. Gapminder[16] on airplane fatalities---v1 is based on[ IATA](http://gapm.io/xiatas), [ICAO[3]](https://www.icao.int/sustainability/Pages/Air-Traffic-Monitor.aspx), [US Air Carrier Safety Data](http://www.gapm.io/xbtsafat) and [Revenue Passenger-Miles](http://www.gapm.io/xbtspass) from BTS[1,2] and The Annual Reports of the U.S. Scheduled Airline Industryfrom [ATAA](http://airlines.org/). See [www.gapm.io/ttranspa](http://www.gapm.io/ttranspa) and [www.gapm.io/dpland](http://www.gapm.io/dpland).
