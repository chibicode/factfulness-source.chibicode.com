Carson's book Silent Spring is definitely one of [the most influential popular science books ever](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Silent_Spring). It sparked a global movement with global impact. Thanks to this movement the number of countries signing international Environmental Treaties is still climbing, as you can see in UNCTAD's treaty collection [visualized at OurWorldInData[9]](https://ourworldindata.org/grapher/number-of-parties-env-agreements).
