To compare dif­ferent kinds of disaster deaths, read ["Not All Deaths Are Equal: How Many Deaths Make a Natural Disaster Newsworthy?"](https://ourworldindata.org/how-many-deaths-make-a-natural-disaster-newsworthy.) from OurWorldInData[8]. Gapminder is currently compiling data about the skewed media coverage of dif­ferent kinds of deaths and dif­ferent kinds of environmental problems. When ready, it will be published here: [www.gapm.io/fndr](http://www.gapm.io/fndr).
