Researchers are trying to figure out how to adjust emissions quotas for changing population sizes; see Shengmin et al. (2011) and Raupach et al. (2014). See [gapm.io/eco2a](http://gapm.io/eco2a).

On Level 4, transportation is behind one third of all CO2 emissions---which also double with income, based on [US EPA data](https://www.epa.gov/ghgemissions/sources-greenhouse-gas-emissions) (27 percent for the US). See [Global, Regional, and National Fossil-Fuel CO2 Emissions](https://data.ess-dive.lbl.gov/#view/ess-dive-ba81a8a8e0bef31-20180330T223152179). For more on CO2 emissions at dif­ferent incomes, visit [gapm.io/tco2i](http://gapm.io/tco2i).
