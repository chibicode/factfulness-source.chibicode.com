Life expectancy data is from [IHME[1]](http://gapm.io/xihlex) (Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation). In 2016, only the Central African Republic and Lesotho had a life expectancy as low as 50 years. But uncertainties are huge, especially on Level 1 and 2. Learn how much data doubt you should have at [www.gapm.io/blexd](http://www.gapm.io/blexd).
